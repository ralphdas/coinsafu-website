FAQ

How does it work?
  1. Write down your recovery words on the conversion sheet.
  2. Find the correlating numbers in the BIP39 word list:https://github.com/bitcoin/bips/blob/master/bip-0039/english.txt
  3. Write down the numbers on the conversion sheet.
  4. Punch the numbers in the boxes of the steel plates. The extra box can be used to number the sequence of your steel plates (1-4)
  5. Lock it and put it somewhere safe.
Can I backup my Trezor, Ledger or KeepKey?
Yes. Each Coinsafu ships with instructions on how to do this. Coinsafu is compatible with all hardware- and software wallets using BIP39.

BIP39?
Your cryptocurrency wallet uses the industry-standard BIP39. This protocol uses backup words that consist of three to eight letters. The first four-letter combinations are unique and sufficient to recover the full word: https://github.com/bitcoin/bips/blob/master/bip-0039/english.txt
